About Us

We are, quite simply, people who like data. Our background is in physics, where large amounts of data are the norm. For over ten years, we have worked with many kinds of data analysis and ways to visualise information. These days techniques in data analysis are spreading from science to society, and concepts like Big Data and Machine Learning have become standard. Trained as researchers, we believe decisions should be made based on the best information available, which requires knowledge of the best techniques. But not everyone has an analytics department on hand, and that’s where we we come in. From making sense of large data volumes to revealing complex but vital correlations, we work with you to find the answers you need. Because your data hold the key, and we like data.

While some might think that each area requires its own specific technique, the surprising thing is that most techniques work equally well regardless of discipline. It doesn’t matter whether you’re trying to optimise prices or proteins, classify naevi or nebulae, or looking for correlations in psychology or physics. The basic data science techniques are universal, and can be applied in any field. What matters is the source and quality of the data, and the question you’re trying to answer. So no matter if you’re in medicine, mechanical engineering or marketing, feel free to get in touch!
